🗓️ IT Leave Support

Taking leave? Let's find your stand-in squad.

Pick your leave day(s) on the calendar and this reads the actual department timetable to tell you — period by period — exactly who's free to cover for you. Multi-hour labs (like 9–11, 11:15–1:15 or 2–4) are read as one full load, and only go to faculty free for every hour of it. Free faculty are also split into two groups: 🟡 those who already teach that exact class (easiest swap — students already know them) and everyone else who's simply free.

1. Plan the leave

Choose yourself, then tap every day you'll be out — right on the calendar. Tap a date again to remove it. Pick a single day, a whole stretch, or a scattered few — no limit on how many.

2. Here's who can cover you

Pinned up, period by period — pick whoever suits and give them a heads up 🙏

Or just peek at one slot

Not planning leave, just want to know who's around on Wednesday 3rd period? Check here — compares every faculty member in one go.

This tool mirrors the standing weekly timetable and repeats it every week — it doesn't know about holidays, exam schedules or one-off changes. Cross-check those separately, and always loop in your HOD for the final word. "Same class" matching is derived automatically from each entry's class/division label in the timetable — please sanity-check unusual cases (combined divisions, honors sessions, cross-branch electives) before finalising a swap.
